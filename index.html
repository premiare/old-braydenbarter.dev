Why?

Great question. Why did I decide to drop my career and move to programmning? Although COVID-19 and 2020 sort of forced my hand on the dropping career part, it was a blessing in disguise.

Late 2019 I had taken up a small interest in programming but didn't have much time to dedicate to it. Along comes 2020 and I had many locked down months to focus purely on coding,
specifically Web Development.

I completed a JavaScript course through LinkedInLearning and moved on to a
Full Stack Web Dev Course through Udemy and Devslopes. After these courses I developed basic websites, which won't see the light of day,
and continued to design and build websites.

My aim is continue learning about Front-end while also learning and practising about back-end, as I have a basic understanding, but definitely not enough to call myself a full-stack developer. The main thing for me to remember is that the end goal is endless, there's always room to learn and improve and that's another thing that attracts me to coding.
